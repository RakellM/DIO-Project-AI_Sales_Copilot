## Delivery Logistics (Canadian Winter Focus)

**Q: How does winter delivery work? It's -20°C outside.**

A: Great question - we've designed for Canadian winters. Our insulated bags maintain food temperature for 2 hours in cold weather. Drivers place bags in the warmest available spot (porch, lobby, or between storm doors). If you're not home, leave a cooler with a heat pack (or we'll text you upon arrival).

**Q: What if my delivery is delayed by a snowstorm?**

A: Safety first - our drivers won't risk unsafe roads. If a storm hits, we'll:
- Text you by 8am with delay notice
- Offer same-day dinner window (5-7pm) instead
- Credit \$10 to your account for the inconvenience
- Cancel and fully refund if no safe delivery possible

**Q: Do you deliver on Canadian holidays?**

A: No delivery on:
- New Year's Day
- Family Day (Ontario, February)
- Good Friday
- Victoria Day
- Canada Day
- Civic Holiday (August)
- Labour Day
- Thanksgiving (October)
- Christmas Day
- Boxing Day

Order cutoff is 2 days before any holiday.

## Emergency & Weather FAQ (Canada)

**Q: What happens during extreme cold warnings (-30°C or colder)?**

A: Customer and driver safety first. If Environment Canada issues an extreme cold warning:
- We text you by 7am with options:
  1. Cancel order (full refund + \$5 credit)
  2. Reschedule to next available warm day (usually within 48 hours)
  3. Pick up from our heated kitchen location
- No penalties, no questions asked

**Q: What about smoke from wildfires (summer 2026)?**

A: If Air Quality Health Index (AQHI) hits 7+ (high risk) in your area, we offer:
- Same-day cancellation with full refund
- Reschedule to following week
- Indoor pickup only (no outdoor delivery until AQHI drops below 7)

We monitor AQHI hourly during wildfire season.

**Q: Do you deliver during Ontario's winter holidays (March break, etc.)?**

A: Yes - we deliver normally during March Break, but families often pause subscriptions. Just set "pause week" in app by the Sunday before.
