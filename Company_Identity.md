# Company Identity

**Name**: TasteCraft


## Core Brand Concept

**TasteCraft** is a premium, ready-to-eat meal service that bridges the gap between scientific nutrition and culinary artistry. Unlike traditional meal prep companies that focus strictly on macros at the expense of flavor, TasteCraft treats nutrition as a craft, ensuring every single meal is chef-driven, vibrant, and genuinely delicious.

## The Core Promises

- **Taste First**: Flavor is never sacrificed for function. The meals are designed to taste like a restaurant experience, not a diet restriction.

- **Never Frozen**: Every meal is delivered fresh, chilled, and ready to heat. This maintains ingredient integrity, texture, and superior flavor compared to frozen options.

- **Universal Nutrition**: Built for everyone. Whether the goal is athletic performance, weight management, or simply convenient healthy eating, the menu adapts to the user.


## Dietary Tracks (The Inclusivity Edge)

While competitors often lean heavily into a single niche (like Keto), TasteCraft uses a multi-track system to capture the entire market:

- **Protein Forward**: High-protein, clean fuel designed for athletes and muscle recovery.

- **GLP-1 Friendly**: Nutrient-dense, portion-controlled, and digestion-supportive meals tailored for those on GLP-1 medications or managing blood sugar.

- **High Fiber**: Plant-rich, gut-healthy options focusing on complex carbohydrates and satiety.

- **Vegetarian**: Creative, chef-crafted plant-based meals that never feel like an afterthought.
