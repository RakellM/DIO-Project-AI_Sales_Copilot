# Example: Post-Purchase Follow-up

## Scenario
Customer placed first order (5 meals). Rep needs to follow up after delivery.

## Sales Rep Query to AI Copilot
"Customer in Hamilton ordered first time - 5 meals, GLP-1 track (Gentle Roots Bowl). Delivered yesterday. Help me follow up."

## AI Copilot Output

## SUGGESTED RESPONSE

"Hi Jennifer! This is [Name] from TasteCraft. I see your first order (Gentle Roots Bowl) was delivered yesterday. How did it go with the ginger-turmeric broth? Many of our GLP-1 customers find sipping the broth first helps settle their stomach before eating the bowl. Reply with:
🍽️ Loved it - no nausea
👍 It was okay - mild symptoms
👎 Made me feel worse

Your feedback helps us improve. Also - if you're enjoying it, I can set up recurring delivery for the same day each week."

## INTERNAL NOTES (Rep Only)

- Customer persona: Persona 2 (GLP-1 User)
- Key need: Check nausea status (critical for retention)
- Recommended next action: If they reply "Loved it" - offer subscription. If "Mild symptoms" - suggest eating slower (20 min) or trying the Mediterranean Cod bowl (gentler). If "Worse" - escalate to manager (potential adverse reaction)
- Watch out for: If they mention vomiting or severe nausea, escalate immediately

## FOLLOW-UP QUESTION (If customer replies positive)

"Would you like me to add the Gentle Roots Bowl to your weekly rotation? We can deliver every Tuesday and Thursday - your choice of lunch or dinner window."

## Expected Customer Response Scenarios

### Scenario A (Positive)
Customer: "Loved it! No nausea at all. The broth really helped."

Rep follow-up (using suggested response above): Offer subscription. Customer converts to Standard subscription (10 meals/week).

### Scenario B (Mild Symptoms)
Customer: "It was okay but I still felt a little queasy after."

Rep follow-up: "Thank you for telling me. Try this next time: Sip the broth over 10 minutes. Then wait 5 minutes. Then eat only half the bowl. Save the rest for an hour later. Many customers find pacing helps. Want me to send written instructions?"

### Scenario C (Negative - Escalate)
Customer: "Made me vomit. This is terrible."

Rep action: ESCALATE TO MANAGER immediately. Do not respond directly. Manager will issue full refund, medical follow-up, and document adverse reaction.
