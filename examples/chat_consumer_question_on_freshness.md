# Example Chat: Customer Freshness Concern

## Scenario
Customer worried about food quality and freshness.

## Sales Rep Query to AI Copilot
"Customer asks: 'How do I know your food is actually fresh? I've tried other services and Day 3 meals were terrible.'"

## AI Copilot Output

## SUGGESTED RESPONSE

"I hear that frustration - really. Most meal companies optimize for shelf life, not flavor. Here's what we do differently:

1. Never frozen - ever. Your meal was cooked within 48 hours of delivery.
2. Component packaging - crispy items (nuts, chips) come sealed separately. Sauces on the side unless you request mixed.
3. 'Day 5 flavor test' - Chef Elena tests every meal on Day 5. If it's not still delicious, it doesn't launch.

I'll give you $15 off your second order if your Day 5 meal disappoints. That's our Fresh Promise."

## INTERNAL NOTES (Rep Only)

- Customer persona: Persona 5 (Skeptical First-Timer) or past competitor user
- Key objection: Trust + past negative experience
- Recommended next action: Offer conditional discount (second order) - this builds confidence without giving away margin on first order
- Watch out for: If they ask about specific competitor names, acknowledge the difference but don't bad-mouth competitors

## FOLLOW-UP QUESTION TO ASK CUSTOMER

"What specifically went wrong with your previous service? Soggy vegetables? Bland sauce? Small portions? I'll tell you exactly how we solve that specific problem."

## Outcome
Customer replies: "Soggy vegetables mostly. Everything was mushy by Day 3."

Rep responds: "Ah - that's from freezing. We never freeze, so our broccoli stays crunchy even on Day 5. Want to try the Iron Craft bowl - the roasted broccoli is our most texture-tested ingredient."

Customer converts. Orders 5 meals.
